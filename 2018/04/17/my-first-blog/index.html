my first blog

Posted on 2018-04-17 | In personal idea
I established my personal website through Hexo and deployed it on Github.

This is my own blog! I would like to write down my feelings at the moment.
One month ago, an idea occured with me —— “I want a real webside which totally belongs to me.”Lacking of implementation capacity, I didn’ turn the idea into action.
However, when I saw a sentence by accident, I was touched and decided to do it.

Start early, move fast, and be bold.

At that moment, I thought that if I could do this in the past, perhaps I could have stronger professional skills and a broader perspective.
Almost all the things I did in the past were futile and inefficient. But now I hope that I can make a change，and start it by insisting on writing the blog.

Go ahead, chase and achieve things. Be a better person.
You’ll find the star that belongs to you. Just like I did.
And you’ll find all the suffers are paid off.

Xin Tong
